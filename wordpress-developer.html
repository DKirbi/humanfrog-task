WordPress Developer

SPRD.digital is looking for a motivated WordPress developer to join our team! We invite anyone who is great at programming and who thrive on developing cleanly written code for websites and who enjoy improving existing sites.

As a developer you will be working closely with our whole development team and our CTO, so that the projects you work on will be completed on time and with an impressive level of quality.

As a company we strive to provide all employees with a peaceful working environment, with unlimited access to coffee, tea, juice, fruit, and other snacks. This job is not remote, but at the office of our sister company, Zabec.net, in Ljubljana.

What We Expect

  • Extensive knowledge of WordPress
  • Precision, dependability, and clear, regular documentation
  • Extensive knowledge of GIT
  • Basic knowledge of HTML/CSS
  • Knowledge of Javascript
  • Knowledge of integration and maintenance for a variety of API connections
  • Quick thinking and reacting in solving problems and bugs that come up on projects
  • Team player, “no problem too big” attitude, time-effective, fluency in English

What We Offer

  • Budget for in-house services
  • Budget for continued education
  • Full-time employment
  • Great working environment
  • Your own computer with multiple screens
  • Chances to test new technologies
  • Collaboration on Slovenian and international projects

Contract type

Full-Time

LOCATION

Ljubljana, Slovenia
